Password safety tips

Keep your rides protected

Your MOTUWA account holds sensitive information: your routes, emergency contacts, and ride reports. A strong password is your first safety shield.

  • Use a unique password. Avoid reusing passwords from other apps or social media.
  • Combine characters: Mix letters, numbers, and symbols to make it harder to guess.
  • Protect your alerts: A secure account ensures only you can trigger or edit emergency notifications.

Once your password is updated, you can log in again, start a new ride, and let MOTUWA keep your loved ones updated every 10 minutes.

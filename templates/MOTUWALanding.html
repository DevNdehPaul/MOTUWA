About MOTUWA

A movement for Safe, dignified public transportation, starting with the most vulnerable riders.

MOTUWA was born from lived experiences of unsafe public rides, late-night commutes, unregistered taxis, and the constant fear that nobody knows where you are. Our mission is simple: to turn every trip into a traceable, accountable, and supported journey.

By combining live tracking, verified driver details, and rapid emergency alerts, we empower riders to claim their right to move freely and safely, while giving families, schools, and cities clear visibility when it matters most.

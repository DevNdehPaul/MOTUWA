Step 2 - Choose how MOTUWA should protect you

MOTUWA can alert your emergency contacts and keep them updated without you entering your live location every time.

You're in control of when alerts go out. Once you start ride alerts, live info and GPS location link is sent to chosen contacts. They receive updated info every 10 minutes until you end the ride or cancel.

What your emergency contacts will see

When you report a ride through MOTUWA, your guardians receive a simple, clear view of your journey and can act quickly if something feels wrong.

  • Instant alert: taxi photo, plate number, driver name, and route are sent as a secure link.

● Reassurance in under 30 seconds

A calm safety check, not an alarm

Quick reports are designed to help you notice how you feel, capture the moment, and quietly keep your support system informed.

  • Check-in only: Use this when everything seems fine but you still want MOTUWA watching over you.
  • Soft alerts: If you choose “Watch closely” or “Alert my people”, your contacts get a gentle update, not a panic message.
  • Full control: You decide when monitoring ends. You can send another quick report at any time.
If you ever feel in immediate danger, you can trigger the full emergency alert from the app for instant, high-priority help.

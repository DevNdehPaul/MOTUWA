Built for safe public transport rides

Your details, their peace of mind

Setting up your profile once means MOTUWA can instantly share your live location, taxi details, and quick safety reports with the people you trust most.

  • Live tracking: Share your route automatically — no need to copy links or send constant updates.

  • 10-minute check-ins: MOTUWA can keep your contacts updated every 10 minutes when monitoring is on.

  • Privacy first: Your information is encrypted and only used to protect you during rides.

You can pause monitoring any time once you arrive safely — MOTUWA works on your terms, at your pace.
